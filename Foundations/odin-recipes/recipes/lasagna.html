Lasagna

Description

A traditional lasagna, with a bout half the time and effort! Serve with garlic toast and salad.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon dried onion flakes
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef over medium-high heat.
  2. Stir in the water and black olives, and season with basil, garlic, oregano, minced garlic, and onion flakes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  4. Place a layer of the meat and sauce in the bottom, and cover with layer of dry noodles.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes in the preheated oven, but check after 30 mins as it depends on how thick is the lasagna.
  6. Lasagna is cooked when you can easily stick a knife into it.
  7. Put cheese over the top and return it to the oven for about 5-10 mins.
  8. Slice & Enjoy!
