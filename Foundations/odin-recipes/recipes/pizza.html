Pizza

Description:

This is a wonderful pizza known as Gourmet White Pizza, It's for those who are looking to get away from the basic, cheese and pepperoni pizza.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pizza Dough
  • Flour
  • Tomato
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Bring out the Yeast from the dough.
  2. Add sugar and warm water on the dough, and let it stand for 5 mins.
  3. Then add flour, oil, salt into the yeast mixture, and beat at medium speed ofr about five minutes.
  4. Toss and Turn the dough.
  5. Put the dough in a bowl, and cover the bowl for 45 mins.
  6. Then cut the dough into four pieces and form into balls.
  7. Cover the dough again and place in the fridge for 30 mins.
  8. Make the Tomato Sauce.
  9. Form the pizza by placing your fingers on both hands together, and go to the edges.
  10. Put the tomato sauce you made on top of the pizza dough, and the Mozzarella.
  11. Bake in the oven for 15 mins.
  12. Slice & Enjoy!
