French Fries

Description

Who doesn't like French Fries. Make restaurant quality fries at home is possible.

Ingredients

  • 800 grams potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into long 8 mm thick french fries.
  2. Soak the fries in water for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat the oil.
  4. Put the fries in the oil, and toss to coat them.
  5. Set a time for about 10-18 mins.
  6. After that slide out the and shake the fries.
  7. Finally, Sprinkle with salt and serve on platter.
  8. Enjoy!
